🕉️ About Lord Shiva

The Supreme Lord of Transformation, Meditation and Divine Grace

Who is Lord Shiva?

Lord Shiva, also known as Mahadev, Bholenath, Shambhu and Maheshwara, is one of the principal deities of Hinduism and a member of the Holy Trinity (Trimurti). He is worshipped as the destroyer of evil, the master of meditation and the source of spiritual awakening. Lord Shiva represents both infinite power and absolute simplicity.

Devotees around the world worship Lord Shiva for peace, wisdom, protection and liberation from worldly suffering.

🔱 Symbols of Lord Shiva

🔱 Trishul

Represents creation, preservation and destruction.

🥁 Damaru

Symbol of cosmic sound and creation.

🌙 Crescent Moon

Represents control over time and mind.

🌊 River Ganga

Symbol of purity and divine blessings.

🐂 Nandi

Represents devotion, loyalty and righteousness.

👁 Third Eye

Represents wisdom and destruction of ignorance.

🕉️ Meaning of Om Namah Shivaya

"Om Namah Shivaya"

One of the most sacred mantras in Hinduism. It means: "I bow to Lord Shiva." Regular chanting of this mantra is believed to bring peace, strength and spiritual growth.
