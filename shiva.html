📖 About Lord Shiva

Lord Shiva, also known as Mahadev, is one of the principal deities of Hinduism and the Supreme Lord of transformation, meditation, compassion and divine power.

He is worshipped as the destroyer of negativity and the source of spiritual awakening.

🔱 Symbols of Lord Shiva

  • Trishul (Trident)
  • Damaru
  • Crescent Moon
  • River Ganga
  • Nandi
  • Third Eye
