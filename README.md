## 🏛️ About Bholenath Dham

Bholenath Dham is a temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, established through devotion, faith and service.

The sacred journey began in January 2025 with the purchase of temple land and culminated in the inauguration of the temple on 14 February 2026.

Today, Bholenath Dham serves devotees through daily worship, religious observances and spiritual activities dedicated to Mahadev.
